Poseidon's Moving Company

Moving out is tough.

Doing it underwater as a shark? That’s even harder.

But you know what’s even more challenging? Working for Poseidon's Moving Company (Pomoco™) and having to juggle all of that — as a shark — under intense time pressure. What are you gonna do? Grab the furniture with your hands?



This project was vital in teaching me how to act as a bridge between an art and a development team. I honestly spent more time integrating and communicating during this project than actually developing, along with filling in as many knowledge gaps (e.g. Particles, audio for the game) as I could.
